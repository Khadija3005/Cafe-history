Most Famous Cafés in History

  • Café Procope — Paris, France
    One of the oldest cafés in Europe and a meeting place for writers and political thinkers.
  • Caffè Florian — Venice, Italy
    Opened in 1720 and famous for its beautiful interior and long cultural history.
  • Café Central — Vienna, Austria
    Popular with poets, artists, and philosophers during the late 1800s.
  • Les Deux Magots — Paris, France
    A well-known meeting spot for famous writers and painters in the 1900s.
