The Story Behind Your Coffee

Cafés are more than just places to grab a drink. For hundreds of years, they have been meeting spots where people share ideas, read, relax, and connect with others. This site walks through how cafés started, how they spread around the world, and why they still matter today.

Start with the origins of cafés, follow a simple timeline of important events, and learn about famous cafés that became part of cultural history. Whether you love coffee or just like hanging out in cozy spaces, café history shows how a simple drink helped shape public life.
